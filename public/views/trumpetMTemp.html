Trumpet Care & Maintenance

1. The most important aspect of care for your trumpet is to be careful. Don’t balance it upright unless you have a stand. Although dings and dents usually don’t affect the playing they aren’t usually not wanted for appearances sake. This video shows how to “take care” of your trumpet.

2. Trumpet assembly. Brass instruments are the easiest to assemble but you still need to be careful when putting in the mouthpiece. Here is a video that shows the proper way to put on your mouthpiece.

3. Oil your valves occasionally when the buttons do not spring back the way they should.

4. Occassionaly grease your valves as needed.
